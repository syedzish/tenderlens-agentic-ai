How to use TenderLens Agentic AI

Review tender documents without losing the bid decision thread.

TenderLens Agentic AI helps a bidding team upload tender files, run a grounded analysis, inspect evidence, discuss the result by text or voice, and export valid briefing files for review.

Open the workspace View example files

What problem does it solve?

Long tender packs become a checklist with evidence, risks, missing documents, and next actions.

What do you get?

A score, checklist, evidence panel, Tender Map, Briefing Deck, questions, live discussion, and exports.

3

Discuss with TenderLens

Discuss the active analysis by text or voice, grounded in the report context.

4

Export and review

Download valid PDF, DOCX, PPTX, TXT, and SVG outputs for team review.
