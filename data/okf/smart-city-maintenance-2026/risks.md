# Delivery And Commercial Risks

The bidder must mobilize within 21 days of award and provide coverage for specialized HVAC systems. A bidder without confirmed subcontractor capacity may face delivery risk.

The 20 percent price weighting means aggressive pricing can help, but weak technical quality can still lose the tender because technical and experience criteria together account for 70 percent.
