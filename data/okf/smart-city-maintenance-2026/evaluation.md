# Evaluation Criteria

The tender allocates 45 percent to technical quality, 25 percent to relevant experience, 20 percent to price, and 10 percent to localization and Arabic reporting capability.

Technical quality includes SLA design, preventive maintenance methodology, IoT monitoring coverage, cybersecurity operating procedures, and reporting quality.
